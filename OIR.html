OIR

Officers Intelligence Rating Test

What is OIR?

OIR is an acronym for Officers' Intelligence Rating. It is the test of the level of intelligence and competence of a candidate. The OIR test basically is a test of verbal and non-verbal reasoning of a candidate.


What to do in OIR?

In OIR you are suppose to answer 40 (sometimes 50 questions) in 17 min of duration. There would be 2 sets each containing 40 questions.


Important points to note

  • 1. You can take out print of the PDF and perform the test in the way it will be held in real, or you can use the pdf and answer from there.
  • 2. Keep timer of 17 min in your mobile device or on any other device and only then start the test. You can use timer given in the left side bottom of the PDF (Click on Start Timer to use the timer).
  • 3. The solutions can be accessed from the links given below the pdf. You can also submit the test to us for reviewing.
