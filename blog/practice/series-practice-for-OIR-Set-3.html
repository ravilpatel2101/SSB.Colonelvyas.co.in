SERIES QUESTIONS SET - 3


Here are some of the best queestions to practice for OIR and Series. Recommended reading Ultimate Guide to Series


1. 2, 6, 4, 4, 6, 2, __ Find the next term.

a) 2


b) 4

c) 6

d) 8


Answer: d) 8
Here there are two series going on simultaneously.
2, 6, 4, 4, 6, 2
Hence, the next term would be 8


2. 1, 2, 6, 24, 120, __ Find the next term.

a) 240


b) 480

c) 720

d) 1440


Answer: c) 720


∴120×6 = 720


3. A, C, C, B, E, ___. Find the next term.

a) E


b) D

c) A

d) B


Answer: c) A
In this series on observing the question we will find two series going on simultaneously.
A, C, C, B, E, ___.
Here the bold letters accounts: A, C, E; It has difference of two letters i.e. A to C skipping B and C to E skipping D.
And the unbold lettes accounts: C, B, __; Here each letter is getting reduced by 1.
∴ Next letter in the series will be A


4. XY_ZXYW_X _ _ Z. Find the next term.

a) WWYZ


b) WZYW

c) YWXZ

d) WZWZ


Xnswer: b) WZYW
In this series, we can break it into 3 terms -
"XY_Z"
"XYW_"
"X__Z"
So we can easily say that every term is suppose to be "XYWZ".
Thus the answer will be:
XY W ZXYW Z X Y W Z == WZYW


5. 36, 24, 12 ___. Find the replacement for (?).

a) 0


b) 2

c) 4

d) 6


Answer: a) 0
In this series -
> 36-12 = 24
> 24-12 = 12
∴ 12-12 = 0


6. 17, 34, 85, 221, __ Find the next term.

a) 578


b) 670

c) 542

d) 841


Answer: a) 578
In this series -
> 17×2 = 34
> 34×2 + 17 = 85
> 85×2 + 34 + 17= 221
> 221×2 + 85 + 34 + 17= 30


7. 381934237, 81934237, 8193423, 193423, ? Find out next term.

a) 8193423


b) 193423

c) 93423

d) 19342


Answer: d) 19342
381934237, 81934237, 8193423, 193423, _______.
The bold number are removed and rest number makes the next term. Hence the answer is 19342.


8. APM_D_PMDD_P_D_A_MDD Find out next terms.

a) MPDAP


b) DAAMAP

c) DAAMDP

d) DMDAMP


Answer: c) DAAMDP
In this series, we can break it into 3 terms -
"APM_D"
"_PMDD"
"_P_D_"
"A_MDD"
So we can easily say that every term is suppose to be "APMDD".
Thus the answer will be:
APM D D A PMDD A P M D D A P MDD == DAAMDP
