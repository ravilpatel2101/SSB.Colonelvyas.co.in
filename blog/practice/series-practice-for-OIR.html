Here are some of the best queestions to practice for OIR and Series. Recommended reading Ultimate Guide to Series


1. 488, 458, 398, 308, 188, ___. Find the next term.

a) 0


b) -12

c) -58

d) -52


Answer: d) -52
> 488-458 = 30
> 458-398 = 60
> 308-188 = 120 and so on...
So by the series next term would be:
188-240 = -52


2. 1728, 864, 432, 216, 108, 54, ___. Find the next term.

a) 27


b) 22

c) 50

d) 12


Answer: a) 27
> 1728÷2 = 864
> 864÷2 = 432 and so on...
So by the series next term would be:
54÷2 = 27


3. A, C, B, D, C, ___. Find the next term.

a) E


b) D

c) A

d) B


Answer: a) E
In this series on observing the question we will find two series going on simultaneously.
A, C, B, D, C, ___.
Here the bold letters accounts: A, B, C.
And the unbold lettes accounts: C, D, __.
So the answer will be E.


4. AB_CABD_A _ _ C. Find the next term.

a) DDBC


b) DCBD

c) BDAC

d) DCDC


Answer: b) DCBD
In this series, we can break it into 3 terms -
"AB_C"
"ABD_"
"A__C"
So we can easily say that every term is suppose to be "ABDC".
Thus the answer will be:
AB D CABD C A B D C == DCBD


5. 625,5,125,25,25, ?,5. Find the replacement for (?).

a) 5


b) 25

c) 125

d) 625


Answer: c) 125
In this series, we can break it into alternate series: 625, 5, 125, 25, 25, __, 5.
Here the numbers written in Bold makes the Sequence: 625, 125, 25, 5
And the numbers unbold makes the Sequence: 5, 25, __.
Hence, the answer will be 25×5 = 125


6. 105,85,60,30,0,-45,-90 Find out the wrong term.

a) -45


b) 60

c) 0

d) -90


Answer: c) 0
In this series -
> 105-85 = 20
> 85-60 = 25 = 20+5
> 60-30 = 30 = 25+5
> 30-0= 30
Hence, 0 is the wrong term here, it should have been -5 then it would become correct.

9. A, B, D, H, __. Find out the next term.

a) L


b) N

c) R

d) P


Answer: d) P
Each letter in the given series is multiplied with 2 to get the next letter in the series.

A × 2 ⇒ 1 × 2 = 2 and the 2nd letter is B,
B × 2 ⇒ 2 × 2 = 4 and the 4th letter is D.
Similarly, H × 2 ⇒ 8 × 2 = 16 and the 16th letter is P.
