What is WAT?

Word Association Test (WAT) is one of the four psychological tests employed by the SSBs in Phase 2 of their testing procedure to evaluate a candidate’s patterns of thought, personality, temperament, achievements, failures and emotional responses based on the principle of his/ her subconscious mind. In this test, a candidate is judged by the psychologist for his/ her OLQs. There are some things therefore, that a defence aspirant must know about WAT before appearing for the SSB Interview.


What to do in WAT?

Just write the first thought which comes to your mind.


Important points to note

  • 1. A total of 15 seconds will be given to the candidates to read a single word and write their response in the form of a complete sentence on their answer sheet.
  • 2. Don't skip any sentence and try writing something instead of noting.
  • 3. Let your sentence be natural and not copied from any source.
  • 4. You can download PDF of all sentences later on from the attempt page. Do not download it before attempting the test.
